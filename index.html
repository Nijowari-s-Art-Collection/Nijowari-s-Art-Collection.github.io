About Me

I'm Kyoki Chiho, an engineer that has decided to become a visual novel writer

Where Eagles Dare to Fly

Developing visual novels, a medium that shines thanks to its impressive diversity, is a challenge few are willing to take.

This is why I decided to join this incredible word and enrich it with my ideas, these being bringing traditional art to the visual novels.

Nijowari: Where Angels Fall is my most ambitious visual novel, containing several original paintings crafted by a very special artist: my aunt.

Now retired from a life of work and parenting, my aunt invests her free time in crafting majestic paintings, most of them abstract.

Interestingly, some of her artwork has been shown in art expositions, fact that proves her worth as an artist. However, an art gallery is nothing compared to the Internet, the last frontier.

This is why I created this page:

  • To expose her art across the globe so people can enjoy traditional art, a medium that should never die.
  • Raise funds to keep these paintings alive and vibrant, the last goal being able to post even more of her compostions.
  • Introduce traditional art to a new brave world, cryptocurrencies and the blockchain being their foundations.

Some of the pieces exposed appear in the game I developed, whereas some others are completely exclusive and not shown anywhere.

Abstract art has multiple readings, which evoke different feelings to different people. Have fun while unraveling the fabrics of Nijowari's complex art.
